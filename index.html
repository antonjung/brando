How it works

Two devices, two roles

Device AThe actor — tap ME
Device BThe reader — scan the QR code

Connecting

  1. On Device A, select a script and tap ME.
  2. A QR code appears — have Device B scan it.
  3. Device B opens the app and connects automatically.
  4. Device A shows "Reader connected!" — tap Enter Audition Mode.

Running the audition

  • Device B sees all script sections listed.
  • Tap a THEM section → Device A's screen clears.
  • Tap a ME section → Device A shows the lines and they scroll at the set speed.

Solo / testing

Tap THEM on any device to enter reader mode locally — no second device needed.
